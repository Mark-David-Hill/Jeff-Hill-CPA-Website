About Us

Jeff Hill, CPA

"Hi. This is Jeff Hill. I moved my office to Bountiful, Utah in 2012. I provide tax consulting, tax preparation & accounting services for small businesses and individuals. I work directly with all of my clients. I don't advertise but have always built my business through referrals. I moved to Bountiful in 1969, graduated from Bountiful High in 1977 and graduated with my Masters Degree in Accounting with an emphasis in Tax From BYU in 1986. Previously I worked at Arthur Young, Ernst & Young & was a partner in Jensen & Keddington, PC. I have over 30 years of experience in all areas of Taxation but now specialize in supporting small businesses and individuals with their tax needs. Let me know if I can help you.
